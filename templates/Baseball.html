Baseball Form Matters

Common Baseball Injuries

Baseball players are susceptible to various injuries due to the repetitive nature of the sport. Some common injuries include:

  • Oblique strains
  • Tendonitis
  • Torn labrum
  • UCL (unilateral collateral ligament) injury
  • Ankle sprains
  • Rotator cuff injuries

Why Proper Form Matters

Form is crucial in baseball for several reasons:

  • Prevents injuries: Proper mechanics reduce the risk of strains, tears, and other injuries.
  • Improves performance: Correct form leads to better hitting, throwing, and fielding.
  • Consistency: Good form ensures consistent results in every play.
